Hi! My name is Natalie (she/her/hers) and I am a sophomore journalism major from Harford County, Maryland. I am a graduate of Edgewood High School's International Baccalaureate program, class of 2020. I now attend the University of Maryland College Park. On campus, I currently serve as a programming co-chair and ambassador for the CIVICUS living and learning program in addition to being a general assignment writer for Stories Beneath The Shell, a mentor for freshman as as part of the University of Maryland's TerraPeer program, and an active member of the Jewish organization MEOR among other clubs. In my free time, I am most likely playing sports such as tennis or basketball or listening to my favorite music artist, Taylor Swift.
