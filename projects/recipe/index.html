Instant Cinnamon Buns

Here is the recipe teaching you how to make amazingly delicious cinnamon buns topped off with a tangy orange glaze.
Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

FOR CINNAMON BUNS YOU WILL NEED:

  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup margarine (melted)

FOR FILLING YOU WILL NEED:

  • 2 tablespoons margarine (melted)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

FOR ORANGE GLAZE YOU WILL NEED:

  • 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Approximately 5 tablespoons of whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons orange zest

This recipe will make a light glaze. Double the sugar content if you desire a thicker glaze.

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Line a square baking dish and set aside.
  3. Combine all ingredients for cinnamon buns into the bowl of your stand mixer. Roll out on a well-floured surface. Roll out into a 10 x 5 inch rectangle.
  4. Melt margarine for the filling and combine brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.
    Using a pastry brush, spread the melted margarine over the dough. Sprinkle the cinnamon filling all over the dough.
  5. Gently roll the dough. Slice into logs, should make about 6 rolls.
  6. Place the rolls into prepared baking dish and lightly brush each roll with the remaining margarine.
  7. Bake for about 15 minutes
  8. Immediately remove the rolls from the pan and transfer to a dish
  9. Combine all ingredients for orange glaze and drizzle over the buns. Best if served warm with a cup of coffee.
